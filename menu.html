Layo Teraga

It's floral, like star jasmine, and with a clarity to the sugary smell of raw honey. Darker roasts have a tropical edge to them, like furit punch. This coffee smells so nice coming out of the grider and even better with some hot water. There's lots of fresh fruit like mango and pineapple, as well as a candied butterscotch note. The cup is juicy and with notes of apricot and floral peach, and lots of sweet, ripe citrus. Orange, pomello, pineapple, mango, lynchee, etc...it's all here! The acidity is integrated and refreshing. It's apparent without being over the top, and reminds me of stone fruit juice. The finish is honeyed and has a pleasing bitter aspect that is like a citrus-peel spritz. Overall, this lot from Layo Teraga is with a sweetness that is seriously like candy.

Nyeri Gatomboya

This is more pungent at darker roast levels with deep flavors of chocolate sauce in the aftertaste.
